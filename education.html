Coming from a mathematical background, I studied statistics before datafication and the rise of big data.
I gained an excellent knowledge of fundamental concepts. I also studied economics and, naturally, econometrics.
Following the 2008 financial crisis, I got interested in finance and passed the first two exams of the CFA.
After a period of skepticism, I understood that data science was not just a rebranding of statistics, and educated myself.
